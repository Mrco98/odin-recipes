Scrambled Egg

Description

Scrambled Egg is a dish that is easy and fast to cook. It is a classic breakfast across different cultures in the world.

As a dish that is easy to make, it is often the first dish that anyone learns to cook. This food provides a good source of protein for people that are looking to improve their health.

Ingredients

  • Raw Eggs
  • Cooking Oil
  • Butter
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Crack the egg into a bowl
  2. Beat the egg until it is mixed up and reached the right consistency
  3. Heat the pan and butter
  4. Add the beated egg to the pan and continuosly mix to avoid overcooking the egg
  5. Add milk to enhance the creaminess of the scrambled egg
  6. Sprinkle the preferred amount of salt and pepper to the egg for enhanced flavors
  7. Take off the pan from the stove and let the egg continue to cook from the pan's heat
  8. Plate the dish and enjoy!
