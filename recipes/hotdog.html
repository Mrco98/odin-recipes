Hotdog

Description

Hotdogs are a part of the classic Filipino breakfast that is loved by people of all ages!

This dish is often paired together with eggs and rice to prepare for the perfect morning.

Ingredients

  • Tender Juicy Classic Hotdog
  • Water
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Thaw the frozen hotdogs
  2. Boil the water with the thawed hotdog
  3. Once there is no more water, heat the pan and cooking oil
  4. Fry the hotdog with the cooking oil until cooked to preference
