French Fries

Description

French fries is a dish that is loved by everybody. Thin cut potatoes that are deef fried in cooking oil and seasoned with salt to perfection.

Who doesn't love potatoes? Fries lover will never get tired of french fries! And potatoes in general.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Fries
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Thaw the fries until it is no longer frozen
  2. Heat the cooking oil in the deef fryer
  3. Deep fry the french fries to perfection!
  4. Salt the fries to your preference
